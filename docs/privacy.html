## Introduction Thank you for using Scanly ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how Scanly collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application. By using Scanly, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. ## Information We Collect ### Camera Access Scanly requires access to your device camera solely for the purpose of scanning QR codes. Camera images and video streams are processed locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers. ### QR Code Content When you scan or generate QR codes, the content may be stored locally on your device as part of your scan history. This information is not uploaded to our servers. ### Usage Data Scanly may collect limited anonymous usage information through analytics services to help improve app performance and user experience. Such information may include: * App version * Device type * Operating system version * Crash reports * General usage statistics This information does not personally identify you. ## How We Use Information We use collected information to: * Provide QR code scanning and generation features * Improve app functionality and performance * Fix bugs and technical issues * Enhance user experience ## Data Storage All scan history and generated QR code data are stored locally on your device unless you choose to share them. We do not maintain servers that store your QR code content. ## Data Sharing We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share your QR code content with third parties. Information may only be disclosed if required by applicable law. ## Children's Privacy Scanly is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. ## Security We take reasonable measures to protect information processed by the App. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. ## Your Rights You may: * Delete your scan history at any time * Uninstall the application at any time * Stop granting camera permissions through your device settings ## Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
